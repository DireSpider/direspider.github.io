The 7.63 GB file has the same video quality but no playbill, skips 1 minute in late Act 1 (A Sentimental Man (Reprise)), skips the first 1.5 minutes of Act 2, and the smalls may glitch such that the last section of Act 1 doesn't play at all. The 4.07 GB file has worse video quality but does have a working menu, and includes the playbill and various extras (TV spots, Macy's special, Seth's Broadway Chatterbox).
